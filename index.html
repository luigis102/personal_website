RAW TRUTH

Luigi Alemanno is a truth seeker (not of the final truth), interested in anything that involves people and nature. He is also fascinated by quantum physics/computing and classical computer science./p>

I was born in southern Italy, where I spent much of my time assisting my grandfather in his window manufacturing business. For four years, I pursued off-road cycling competitions, balancing intense training and races with work in the family business, which left little time for school. Cycling placed me in the most demanding mental and physical challenges I have ever faced, teaching me that no effort is ever truly "enough." I dedicated nearly all my time to the sport, though I ultimately realized that a world-class career in that domain was not what I really wanted./p>
